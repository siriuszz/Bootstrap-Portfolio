About Me

I moved to Atlanta three years ago from North Carolina where I led the digital marketing program for an academic medical center. In 2012, I earned my MBA from Wake Forest University.

Since arriving here, I've done some acting and writing, produced an independent film, and even dabbled in stand-up comedy. But the part of my brain that loves logic problems and and the other part of my brain that likes actually making a living conspired to pull me back to the digital world.
